About

Venue - City of London

Urban coding will be hosted at the ExCeL Centre in the UK's number one city of London.

Believe your skills are street enough to deliver ground breaking work, yes or no Urban Coding is the place for you. You will have access to industry leading experts to guide you through the techiques in the most popular languages to deliver these results.

Why Urban Coding

A gap in the market was established in the early 2000's where a quantum shift was needed from the static environment of desktop browsing with the need to cross platforms to mobile.

The Urban coder was born with their ability to think outside the box and creativity that simply was not needed before. If you are passion about developing tech and pushing the boundaries of the rules of coding then you are urban coder and will be grossed with the events line up.

Guest Speakers

All our speakers have been there and lead on the ever change face of the internet. Their passion deliver of tools that they use in the languages we all know will give you that edge to take your creations to the next level and an opportunity to become a true urban coder.

Urban Coding is committed to being inclusive and welcoming for everyone. We look forward to another intensive day of learning and sharing.

What You'll Learn

MongoDB: NoSQL database Express: Framework for Node React: JavaScript library Node.js: JavaScript environment ES2015: Latest version of JavaScript Babel: JavaScript compiler

Speakers

Vivianne McVue

NodeStradamus

Robbie Redux

Ecma Scriptnstuff

Jay Query

Json Babel
